# Isomorphism
$f: G_1 \rightarrow G_2$ which is bijective such that is preserves the Binary Operation. 

**Homomorphism Property**
$f(x \cdot y) = f(x) \cdot f(y)$ where the Left Hand Side is $G_1$ and the right hand side is in $G_2$.
