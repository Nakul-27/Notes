# Slope
## Mathematics: "Rate of Change"
### Used for Max/Min
#### $dy/dt = 0$: No Change
#### $dy/dt > 0$: Increasing
#### $dy/dt < 0$: Decreasing
If $dy/dt = y$ then the following is true:
Equilibrium: $y = 0$
Sol'n Increasing: $y > 0$
Sol'n Decreasing: $y < 0$
